Jerarquía de operaciones

Video

Ejemplo

Resuelve aplicando la jerarquía de operaciones:

$$\begin{aligned} -5+6(-3+8)-4(-3)(2)-5(-3)^2 &= \\ -5+6(5)+24-5(9) &= \\ -5+30+24-45 &= \\ -50+54 &= \mathbf{4} \end{aligned}$$
$$\begin{aligned} 2[-5(-3(-2+8))] &= \\ 2[-5(-3(6))] &= \\ 2[-5(-18)] &= \\ 2[90] &= \mathbf{180} \end{aligned}$$

Respuestas

▼
Bloque 1
$$\begin{aligned} 4-2(-1)(-5)+6(-1+4)-3(-5)^2 &= \\ 4-10+6(3)-3(25) &= \\ 4-10+18-75 &= \\ 22-85 &= \mathbf{-63} \end{aligned}$$
$$\begin{aligned} 5(-2)(3)+4(-1-5)-3+4(-1)^2 &= \\ -30+4(-6)-3+4(1) &= \\ -30-24-3+4 &= \\ -57+4 &= \mathbf{-53} \end{aligned}$$
$$\begin{aligned} 5[-3(4(-1-2))] &= \\ 5[-3(4(-3))] &= \\ 5[-3(-12)] &= \\ 5[36] &= \mathbf{180} \end{aligned}$$
$$\begin{aligned} -3[2(-5(-4+10))] &= \\ -3[2(-5(6))] &= \\ -3[2(-30)] &= \\ -3[-60] &= \mathbf{180} \end{aligned}$$
Bloque 2
$$\begin{aligned} 8-6(3-4)-2(3)^2+2(6)(-1) &= \\ 8-6(-1)-2(9)-12 &= \\ 8+6-18-12 &= \\ 14-30 &= \mathbf{-16} \end{aligned}$$
$$\begin{aligned} 3(-2)^4+6-3(-4)(-1)+7(-3+10) &= \\ 3(16)+6-12+7(7) &= \\ 48+6-12+49 &= \\ 103-12 &= \mathbf{91} \end{aligned}$$
$$\begin{aligned} 4[-3(2(-10+4))] &= \\ 4[-3(2(-6))] &= \\ 4[-3(-12)] &= \\ 4[36] &= \mathbf{144} \end{aligned}$$
$$\begin{aligned} -6[5(-4(3+7))] &= \\ -6[5(-4(10))] &= \\ -6[5(-40)] &= \\ -6[-200] &= \mathbf{1200} \end{aligned}$$
