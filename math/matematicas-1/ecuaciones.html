Ecuaciones de primer grado

Video

Ejemplo

Resuelve las siguientes ecuaciones de primer grado:

$$\begin{aligned} 3+4(2x-1) &= 1-2(3-5x) \\ 3+8x-4 &= 1-6+10x \\ 8x-10x &= 1-6+4-3 \\ -2x &= -4 \\ x &= \tfrac{-4}{-2} \\ \mathbf{x} &= \mathbf{2} \end{aligned}$$
$$\begin{aligned} 2(x-3)+7 &= 6x-5(1+2x) \\ 2x-6+7 &= 6x-5-10x \\ 2x-6x+10x &= -5+6-7 \\ 6x &= -6 \\ x &= \tfrac{-6}{6} \\ \mathbf{x} &= \mathbf{-1} \end{aligned}$$

Ejercicios

Bloque 1
$6-5(3x-1)=-9+5(4x+2)$
$6(x+2)-3=4x-2(4-5x)$
$-2+3(4+7x)=1-3(x-6)$
Bloque 2
$2(-5x+7)+9=8x-4(1+6x)$
$3x-4(x-1)=5(2x+3)-10$
$-3(-2x-8)+6=7x+5(4-2x)$

Respuestas

▼
Bloque 1
$$\begin{aligned} 6-5(3x-1) &= -9+5(4x+2) \\ 6-15x+5 &= -9+20x+10 \\ -15x-20x &= -9+10-6-5 \\ -35x &= -10 \\ x &= \tfrac{-10}{-35} \\ \mathbf{x} &= \mathbf{\tfrac{2}{7}} \end{aligned}$$
$$\begin{aligned} 6(x+2)-3 &= 4x-2(4-5x) \\ 6x+12-3 &= 4x-8+10x \\ 6x-4x-10x &= -8-12+3 \\ -8x &= -17 \\ x &= \tfrac{-17}{-8} \\ \mathbf{x} &= \mathbf{\tfrac{17}{8}} \end{aligned}$$
$$\begin{aligned} -2+3(4+7x) &= 1-3(x-6) \\ -2+12+21x &= 1-3x+18 \\ 21x+3x &= 1+18+2-12 \\ 24x &= 9 \\ x &= \tfrac{9}{24} \\ \mathbf{x} &= \mathbf{\tfrac{3}{8}} \end{aligned}$$
Bloque 2
$$\begin{aligned} 2(-5x+7)+9 &= 8x-4(1+6x) \\ -10x+14+9 &= 8x-4-24x \\ -10x-8x+24x &= -14-9-4 \\ 6x &= -27 \\ x &= \tfrac{-27}{6} \\ \mathbf{x} &= \mathbf{-\tfrac{9}{2}} \end{aligned}$$
$$\begin{aligned} 3x-4(x-1) &= 5(2x+3)-10 \\ 3x-4x+4 &= 10x+15-10 \\ 3x-4x-10x &= 15-10-4 \\ -11x &= 1 \\ \mathbf{x} &= \mathbf{-\tfrac{1}{11}} \end{aligned}$$
$$\begin{aligned} -3(-2x-8)+6 &= 7x+5(4-2x) \\ 6x+24+6 &= 7x+20-10x \\ 6x-7x+10x &= 20-24-6 \\ 9x &= -10 \\ \mathbf{x} &= \mathbf{-\tfrac{10}{9}} \end{aligned}$$
